What is Hunter?

In Hunter × Hunter, a Hunter is a licensed elite capable of entering restricted areas and handling dangerous tasks such as discovering rare creatures, exploring unknown regions, and pursuing criminals.


To earn this status, candidates must pass the extremely harsh Hunter Examination, which has a success rate of less than one in a hundred thousand. Those who pass receive a Hunter License, granting broad privileges and access to exclusive information.


  • 1-Star Hunter: Outstanding achievements in a specific field
  • 2-Star Hunter: Holds an official position and mentors another Hunter to the 1-star level
  • 3-Star Hunter: Exceptional accomplishments across multiple fields, recognized as top-tier
